The Imole Osun Technovation project is aimed at driving innovation with technology in Osun state leveraging various digital platforms to build productive capacities of Osun youth and invariably making Osun become a SMART State using relevant indicators.

Imole Osun Technovation is a proposed episodic project aimed at sustainable transformation of governance landscape in Osun state. The project is designed to facilitate the digitization of processes and actions of the government as a pathway to modernization of service delivery to the people of Osun state.

The project focuses strategically on the youth empowerment and activation of talents that can support the media engagements of the Governor at the grassroot level by leveraging digital skills.

The spatial distribution of communities in Osun state which hitherto hinders their connection to development at the centre shall be mitigated through this project as participants shall be recruited across the 30 local governments of the state and the state area office based on merit.

The selected candidates for this pilot program will be exposed to training in the field of technology and innovation of diverse but relevant digital skills ranging from project designs, product development and cybersecurity among others to model for the decent job of the future as encapsulated in the Sustainable development goals of the United Nations.

  1. inclusivity of the Osun state youth in the globally increasing digital market
  2. To promote the application of digital technology in Osun state as part of dividends of good and smart governance
  3. TO increase tech space employability for the citizens of Osun state
  4. To improve Youth Empowerment and Employment
  5. TO bridge the information and communication gap between the people and government
